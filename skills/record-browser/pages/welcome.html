HelixLab Recorder

Ready to Capture

Record user workflows, annotate key moments, and let your AI agent analyze the results. Navigate to any page using the address bar, then start recording.

Quick Start
1
Navigate to your target page using the browser address bar
2
Record — click Record in the toolbar below, or On Action to start on your next click
3
Annotate — press D or click Draw to circle, arrow, or label things for the AI to focus on
4
Pause with Space to add annotations without them appearing mid-recording
5
Stop when done — save, rename, or start over from the dialog
Drawing Tools
✎
6 tools: Select, Pen, Line, Rectangle, Circle, Text — click Draw or press D to enter draw mode
●
5 colors: Red, Yellow, Blue, Green, White — press 1-4 to quick-switch, all optimized for AI visibility
↗
Lines & Arrows: Plain line, arrow end, arrow start, or double arrow — select style before or after drawing
▤
Stroke & Fill: 4 stroke widths (1/2/3/5px), toggle semi-transparent fill on shapes and text backgrounds
T
Text: 4 sizes (S/M/L/XL), background toggle (BG), border toggle (BD) — click to place, double-click to re-edit
How Annotations Work
Annotations are tied to the recording timeline. They appear in the video from the moment you draw them and stay visible until you move, resize, or delete them. Pause the recording to reposition annotations between scenes — the AI agent will see exactly what was on screen at each point in the recording.
